Member

MODA members will get access to partner offers and can earn rewards and trade liquidity pools. Members can receive NFT Drops and Token Drops from new artists and creators who join the alliance and new projects who choose MODA.

Ambassador

MODA Ambassadors can pitch their own proposals for community voting and can access rewards at a higher level. New projects and partners must have at least one Ambassador on their team.

Creator

Creatives do more with MODA. It’s simple, our creators were amongst the first in the world to put their work on-chain. MODA Creators get access to the platforms and products within the group and use $MODA tokens to have a say.

Board

The MODA board is made up of representatives from across the creative landscape, including strategic investors.The MODA Board are the steering body until community DAO takes over.
